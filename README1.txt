NUNTIAUM
NUNTIAUM IS A COMMUNICATION APPLICATION WHICH FACILITATES USERS TO COMMUNICATE WITH EACH OTHER.
A RESGISTERED USER CAN COMMUNICATE WITH OTHER INDIVIDUALLY AND VIA GROUP.
A MEMBER CAN LOGIN-IN IN THE SYSTEM.
A USER CAN REGISTER ON THE APPLICATION.
THRER ARE TWO TYPES OF MESSAGES IN THE SYSTEM:
A) UNICAST MESSAGES.
B) MULTICAST MESSAGES.
A GROUP CAN MULTIPLE MODERATORS.
A MODERATOR CAN ADD AND REMOVE MEMBER.
